Dog Sitting Site Plan

Daniele Newby

03 - Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide helpful information and services about dog sitting. It will help pet owners learn what I offer, how I care for dogs, and how to contact me for bookings.

Audience

My site is made for dog owners who need a safe and loving place for their pets while they are away. It is for local families and individuals who want someone they can trust with their dogs.

04 - Dynamic Elements

I will use JavaScript to add interactive features such as showing or hiding service details, a gallery filter, and input checking for a contact form. It will use DOM updates, events, objects, functions, and array methods.

05 - Logo

Logo will be a paw print design with the site name "Loving Paws Dog Sitting."

06 - Colors & Fonts

Primary Color: #4BA3C3 (blue)
Accent Color: #F4A261 (orange)
Background: #F7FAFC or white
Fonts: Poppins or Montserrat (headings), Roboto (body text)

07 - Content

Home Page

  • Why pets love staying here
  • Dog photo grid

Child Page - Sitter

  • About me: my animal experience
  • How I take care of pets
  • Feeding, safety, play
  • Gallery of photos
